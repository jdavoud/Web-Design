Crunchball 3000


By djstatika

View all games by this author »

Description

Futuristic violent sports game. Simple rules: 2 teams of 10. Get the ball in the opponents goal.

Play your way up through 4 divisions to become world champions.

32 Teams
320 Players each with their own unique names and abilities
Customize all team colors, names and player names
Train your players, upgrade their equipment, transfer players and even give them performance enhancing drugs at the risk of a fine

Inspired by the classic amiga game "Speedball 2: Brutal Deluxe"

Note: Game runs quicker in Firefox

Download this handy software to allow you to play Crunchball 3000 (and other flash games) with a joypad:
http://electracode.com/4/joy2key/JoyToKey%20English%20Version.htm

Version 1.1
Auto player switch now disabled by default (can be switched on in options menu) – press pass key when you don’t have the ball to switch to the nearest player
Holding down the tackle button will no longer throw the ball accidentally as soon as you get it, you must press it again to throw
You can no longer get infinite money by repeatedly reloading the game after buying upgrades

Team star rating now accurate

+ Other minor fixes

Controls

Re-definable via options menu

p - Pause

Player 1 Controls

WASD - Movement
G - Throw ball
H - Pass to highlighted player
J - Change formation

Player 2 Controls

Arrows - Movement
NUM 1 - Throw ball
NUM 2 - Pass to highlighted player
NUM 3 - Change formation
